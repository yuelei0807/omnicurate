# Business Context

You are supporting the data-management team for OmniRetail, a mid-size retailer preparing for a customer-360 and order-reconciliation initiative.

The raw source files contain customers, products, orders, payments, and support tickets from disconnected operational systems. The business suspects there are duplicate customers, inconsistent geography values, invalid foreign keys, payment mismatches, malformed timestamps, and order-quality issues.

Your goal is to use an agentic coding assistant such as Claude Code, Cursor, Windsurf, or GitHub Copilot to build a small, reproducible local data-management solution that ingests the raw data, creates curated tables, runs data-quality checks, and produces a concise quality and analytics report.

Use local execution only. Do not use paid cloud services.
